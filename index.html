Welcome to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham!

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a premier educational institution known for its excellence in teaching, research, and innovation. Founded in 1994 by Mata Amritanandamayi Devi, it has five campuses across India and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields.
